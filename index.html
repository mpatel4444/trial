About Us

The Matliwala Public School which was started under the aegis of the Matliwala Education Society is situated on a pristine atmosphere on the banks of river Narmada at Bharuch, Gujarat. The school runs classes from LKG – XII offering both the commerce and science streams at the Senior Secondary Levels. Since its inception in 2004 the school has grown over the years. The school has a total strength of 1400 children. The school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education. New Delhi.

Our Mission

The Matliwala Public School has been established with the objective of promoting education among one and all. I personally believe that education in its true sense is a pre-requisite in enhancing the progress of mankind. The mission of the school is to make the students component for the modern world and at the same time shape their character righteously. Thus to ensure the dual objective, we have adopted CBSE syllabus on one hand and have introduced Islamic studies an integral part in the course of studies in our school. More ever, our students are getting an international exposure through the foreign teachers who visit our school every year.

Our Vision

The Matliwala Public School declares it’s Mission to instill in learners a sense of God and Human consciousness, a passion for acquiring Knowledge and a commitment to reach full potential throughout life. Towards attaining this we shall endeavour: To provide learning experiences which are holistic, heuristic, relevant, challenging, dynamic and fulfilling, aimed at a balanced growth of personality, and encouraging freedom of thought and expression within the bounds of Shariah; To create opportunities and inculcate a yearning amongst learners and educators to excel in every endeavour; To develop in learners the qualities of compassion, fellowship, loyalty, decision making and leadership To provide an environment which promotes an awareness and understanding of the conditions in society, which will contribute to its growth and development
